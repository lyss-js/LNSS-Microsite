How it works

We’ll reach out to you to make an appointment with a local LNSS Preferred contact lens specialist near you

The LNSS Preferred doctor will test your eyes and make sure you're a perfect candidate for contact lenses.

Our trained lens fitters will help you try on your lenses and spend enough time with you to be sure you’re ready to do it on your own.

You’ll be enrolled in our lens success program to help you adjust to your new lenses. Our staff and technicians will be standing by ready to answer questions or give you advice.

Mythbusting 101

Is it going to get stuck in my eye?

This is very unlikely. Contact lenses can usually be removed easily using the method your eye doctor recommended. All it takes is practice and patience.

Is it really comfortable?

About 45 million people in the United States think so! But if yours aren't, the first thing to do is reach out to your eye doctor for help. They'll be happy to find a lens that fits you better.
