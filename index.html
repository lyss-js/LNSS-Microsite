Mythbusting 101

Is it going to get stuck in my eye?

This is very unlikely. Contact lenses can usually be removed easily using the method your eye doctor recommended. All it takes is practice and patience.

Is it really comfortable?

About 45 million people in the United States think so! But if yours aren't, the first thing to do is reach out to your eye doctor for help. They'll be happy to find a lens that fits you better.

Can I get colored contact lenses?

Yes! Define enhances your eyes natural beauty. These lenses blend seamlessly with your own eye color. However, ACUVUE does not make tinted lenses.

Can I wear them if I have astigmatism?

Yes. Astigmatism is no longer a barrier to wearing contact lenses. We have several contact lenses that correct for astigmatism.

Will it infect my eye?

Contact lens-related health problems can occur, but they're rare. Infections are most commonly related to poor cleaning routines or other lens-care-related issues. Follow your eye doctor's directions for proper wear and care and you should be fine.

Is it bad to sleep in them?

It is not safe to sleep while wearing contact lenses. Always remove your lenses before sleeping.
